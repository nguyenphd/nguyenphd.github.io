Biography

My name is Phuong-Duy Nguyen (Jay), currently a M.Sc. student in Global Environmental Economics at University of Galway, Ireland. Previously, I am a researcher and consultant specialising in agrifood and environmental economics in the Mekong Delta region of Vietnam. I have 6+ years of experience working across academia, business chamber, and public research institution.

I am open to opportunities involving research collaboration, fieldwork and project consulting, with a focus on the Mekong Delta region and broader efforts in sustainable development across Vietnam. For further information, please contact me via [phuongduy.nguyen@gmail.com].

Education

  • M.Sc. in Global Environmental Economics (2026), University of Galway, Ireland.
  • B.Sc. in Natural Resource Economics (2018), Can Tho University, Vietnam.

Research Interests

  • Agri-Food Economics: Food choice, food consumption, agri-food value chains.
  • Environmental Economics: Economics of climate resilience & adaptation, economic valuation of environmental externalities, circular economy.
  • Urban Studies: Urban development, ecosystem services in cities.
