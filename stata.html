Data set

Data used in this section is extracted from Vietnam PAPI (Public Administratation and Performance Index) website (https://papi.org.vn/) in 2014-2023 period.

Available here: PAPI-2014to2023

Set up

To install the grstyle package, type:

.    ssc install grstyle, replace

To install the palettes package, type:

.    ssc install palettes, replace

To customize the overall look of graph, type:

.    grstyle init ///
>    grstyle color background white ///
>    grstyle color major_grid dimgray /// 
>    grstyle linewidth major_grid thin ///
>    grstyle yesno draw_major_hgrid yes ///
>    grstyle yesno grid_draw_min yes ///
>    grstyle yesno grid_draw_max yes ///
>    grstyle anglestyle vertical_tick horizontal

Some basic commands

To generate variable, type:

.    gen var1=n 
.    gen var1=n if var2=="" 
.    gen var1=n if inlist(var2, "", "", "") 
.    gen var1=1+9*[[var2-var3]/[var4-var5]]  
.    bysort var1: gen rank=_n
.    egen var1 = min(var2), by(var3) 
.    egen var1 = max(var2), by(var3)

To label variable, type:

.    label define label_name 1 "" 2 "" 3 "" ///
>    label values var1 label_name ///

To sort in ascending or descending order, type:

.    gsort var1 
.    gsort -var1 
.    gsort var1 -var2

Graphs

Example 1:

.    graph bar unweighted if pro=="cantho", ///
>    over(year) blabel(bar, format(%9,2f) orientation(vertical) position(inside) color(black)) ///
>    title(`"{bf:KẾT QUẢ ĐÁNH GIÁ PAPI GIAI ĐOẠN 2014-2023}"' `"————————————————"' `"{bf: THÀNH PHỐ CẦN THƠ}"') ///
>    ytitle("") bargap(5) bar(1, fcolor(226 191 217) lcolor(black)) text(50 4 "#55", size(small)) ///
>    text(50 14 "#2", size(small)) text(50 24 "#1", size(small)) text(50 34 "#11", size(small)) ///
>    text(50 45 "#8", size(small)) text(50 55 "#8", size(small)) text(50 65 "#29", size(small)) ///
>    text(50 75 "#42", size(small)) text(50 85 "#55", size(small)) text(50 95 "#53", size(small))

Example 2:

.    graph hbar unweighted if year==2023 & rank<=10, ///
>    over(id, sort(1) descending) blabel(bar, format(%9,2f)) ysc(r(. 5)) ylabel(#5, nogrid labsize(*0.6)) ///
>    ysize(6) bar(1, color(cranberry)) title("{bf:TOP 10 - PAPI NĂM 2023}") ///
>    ytitle("") graphregion(margin(l+5 r+5))

Example 3:

.    graph bar dim1 dim11 dim12 dim13 dim14 ///
>    if pro=="cantho", over(year) blabel(bar, format(%9,2f) orientation(vertical) size(vsmall) pos(outside) color(black)) ///
